Did you know that the orange fruit is as old as 4000BC? Also, the fruit came before the color did! Oranges are a great source of Vitamin C. probably the most popular and widely used fruit in juice form.

Orange juice is known to control certain respiratory diseases . A fresh glass of orange juice after a workout can help replace the minerals and supply the required vitamins the body needs, especially after losing water during the workout. Consuming a whole orange can provide up to 85 calories. The white portion between the skin and the inner flesh is called as pith and is the part which carries most of the fiber in the orange. Consuming the inner orange petals with the pith is highly recommended.

When we squeeze a best quality organic orange for its juice, we usually cut it into half and squeeze it until all the juice falls out. But if all the benefits of the orange have to be utilized, it is recommended to squeeze an entire orange and then make a pulp of it in a grinder. This does make the juice slightly bitter but this is much healthier than the regular orange juice.
